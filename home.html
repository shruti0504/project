Tips

Tips

  • Plan Ahead: Ensure you pack enough reusable products to last the duration of your trip.
  • Stay Organized: Use labeled pouches or bags to keep clean and used items separate.
  • Stay Informed: Research the availability of washing facilities and water quality at your travel destination.
  • Hygiene First: Always wash your hands before handling menstrual products.

Eco-Friendly Products

Eco-Friendly Products

  • Biodegradable Pads: Made from natural materials, decomposes quickly.
  • Organic Tampons: Free from chemicals and pesticides, made from organic cotton.
  • Reusable Panty Liners: Washable and reusable, made from organic materials.
  • Period Underwear: Absorbent and reusable, reduces waste significantly.
  • Reusable Heat Pads: For menstrual cramps, can be reused multiple times.
