• Myths vs. Facts

    Women should avoid exercise during their period.

    Exercise can reduce menstrual symptoms and is beneficial during periods.

    Period blood is dirty.

    Period blood is not dirty;It includes a mix of blood, tissue from the lining of the uterus, and vaginal secretions.

    Using tampons can cause loss of virginity.

    Virginity is a social and cultural concept.Using tampons does not affect virginity. Tampons are a safe menstrual product choice.

    You can't get pregnant during your period.

    It is possible to get pregnant during your period, though the chances are lower.

    Period pain is always normal

    Severe period pain is not normal can indicate conditions like endometriosis or fibroids.It should be discussed with a healthcare provider.

    Irregular periods are always a sign of a health problem

    Irregular periods can be caused by various factors such as stress, diet, exercise, and hormonal changes. However, persistent irregularity should be discussed
